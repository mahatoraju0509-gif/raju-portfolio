About Me

20+ Years Young

Raju Mahato (also known as Raju Mahato Nuniya / Digambar Mahato) is a passionate Nepalese youth leader, self-taught web developer, AI learner, and social activist currently based in Japan. Originally from Yamunamai-1, Rautahat, Nepal, he represents a new generation of independent youth combining technology, leadership, and social impact.

Despite coming from a non-traditional technical background, he has successfully developed real-world websites, learned advanced digital tools through self-study, and built a growing identity in web development and AI-assisted systems. As the Central Executive Committee Member and Madhesh Province In-Charge of Sahid Tatha Bepatta Yoddha Santati Foundation, he has been instrumental in advocating for martyr families across Nepal.
